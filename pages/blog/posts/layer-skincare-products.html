How to Layer Skincare Products

When layering, the general rule is to start with lighter, water-based products and move to heavier, oil-based ones. Follow these tips:

  • Wait for Absorption: Allow each product to absorb fully before applying the next to avoid pilling.
  • Use a Pea-Sized Amount: Less is more when it comes to serums and moisturizers. Overloading your skin can reduce effectiveness.

Nighttime Skincare Layering Checklist

Follow this checklist for your nighttime routine:

  1. Cleanser: Did you remove all makeup and impurities?
  2. Toner: Did you tone your skin to prepare it for treatments?

  4. Eye Cream: Did you hydrate your eye area gently?
